What is The Alter?

"The Alter" eliminates the software product's inconsistency by keeping the SDLC's best practices and ensuring that the final product has followed the principles of HCI (Human-Computer Interaction) with minimal supervisor guidance under the COVID 19 scenario.

Main Components of the System:

  • Generate UML diagrams (Activity Diagram, Sequence Diagram, Use Case Diagram, Class Diagram).
  • Obtain generated UML diagrams and converted them into low-fidelity prototypes.( Wireframe).
  • Obtain generated low-fidelity prototype (wireframe) and converted them into direct web interfaces.
  • Finally, check whether proper HCI principles have been applied in the final product.

Consequently, this solution fundamentally satisfies the semi-automated SDLC system based on HCI for academic students and software developers (both trainees and employees) who are unable to attend their workplace or university work as a team.
